# UI/UX Research for AI Agent Interaction
## Description
Conduct systematic research to define the optimal user interface and interaction patterns for AI agents, reducing developer friction and addressing the currently undefined UI/UX challenge.
## Acceptance Criteria
- Interview at least 20 developers and end‑users about their expectations for AI agent interfaces.
- Produce a documented set of UI/UX standards and prototypes.
- Validate prototypes with a usability test achieving a satisfaction score of >80%.
## Definition of Done
- Research report and UI/UX guidelines are completed.
- Prototypes are implemented in a demo environment.
- Findings are shared with the product and engineering teams.
## Metadata
- **Priority:** high
- **Category:** research & design
- **Risk:** Without clear UI, adoption may stall, reducing market impact.
- **Dependencies:** none
- **Estimate:** M
