# Cost Reduction Milestones for Inference
## Description
Establish and publicly track concrete milestones to achieve at least 100× lower inference cost, aligning with the organization’s deflationary narrative and encouraging ecosystem builders.
## Acceptance Criteria
- Define baseline inference cost metrics for current models.
- Publish a roadmap with quarterly cost‑reduction targets leading to 100× reduction.
- Implement monitoring dashboards showing real‑time cost per token.
- Achieve the first 10× reduction milestone within six months.
## Definition of Done
- Milestone roadmap approved by product leadership.
- Dashboards integrated with engineering telemetry.
- Public announcement prepared and released.
## Metadata
- **Priority:** high
- **Category:** performance & cost
- **Risk:** Failure to meet cost targets may undermine the deflationary promise and deter developers.
- **Dependencies:** none
- **Estimate:** M
